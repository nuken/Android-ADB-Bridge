# Setting Up Android TV Developer Connection

To allow your PC to tune channels and control your Android streaming stick, you must enable Developer Options and authorize the connection.

### Step 1: Unlock Developer Mode
1. Navigate to the home screen of your Android TV stick.
2. Select the **Gear icon (Settings)** in the top right corner.
3. Go to **System** (or **Device Preferences**), then select **About**.
4. Scroll down to highlight **Android TV OS build** (or simply **Build**).
5. Press the **Select/OK** button on your remote **7 times quickly**. 
6. A notification will appear stating: *"You are now a developer!"*

### Step 2: Enable Debugging
1. Press the **Back** button to return to the System menu.
2. Scroll down and open the newly visible **Developer options** menu.
3. Find **USB debugging** (or Network debugging) and toggle it **ON**.

### Step 3: Authorize the PC
1. Add the device IP to your PC dashboard to initiate the first connection.
2. Look at your TV screen. A prompt will appear asking to **Allow USB debugging?**
3. **CRITICAL:** Check the box for **Always allow from this computer**.
4. Select **Allow**.

*Note: If you do not check "Always allow", the background service will fail to reconnect if the TV stick reboots.*
